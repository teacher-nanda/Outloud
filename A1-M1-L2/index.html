BEAT 3 · BUILD The four sentences you need

Ask, and ask again

These four sentences will save you in every hotel, every airport and every phone call.

How do you spell it?

You ask them.

It's N as in November.

When they hear the wrong letter.

Sorry, can you repeat that?

Always polite. Always works.

Can you say it more slowly, please?

The most useful sentence in this course.

💭 Check your understanding
  • Somebody says your name wrong. What do you say? (It's ___ as in ___)
  • Somebody speaks too fast. What do you say? (Can you say it more slowly, please?)

HW3 Your AI Partner Card

🤖 Spelling drill, ten minutes

Open ChatGPT, Claude or Gemini. Copy this in exactly.

You are my English teacher. My level is A1 — I am a beginner. Give me 15 words to spell, ONE AT A TIME. Wait for my answer each time. Start with 3-letter words and get longer. Use only everyday words. I will type the letters separated by spaces, like: C A T After each answer, tell me only "Correct" or "Not quite" and show the right spelling in bold. Do not explain anything else. At the end, tell me which letters I got wrong most often, and give me 5 more words that practice exactly those letters. Start with word 1 now.
